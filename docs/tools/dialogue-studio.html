💡 Tips for describing a conversation
  • Just write what each character says, in their voice. I'll format it, add the camera/gestures, and split it into the game's nodes.
  • Note any player choices — e.g. "Player can say: 'I'll take the shift' or 'Maybe later'" — and what each leads to. Optional; skip if it's a straight conversation.
  • You can ignore the mechanics. Each card already lists "What should change in-game" (rewards, unlocks, flags) — I'll wire those in. Only mention them if you want something different.
  • Don't worry about length or polish. A few sentences of intent is enough; bullet points are fine.
  • The grey starter text is just a suggestion — rewrite it however you like, or clear it and start fresh.
