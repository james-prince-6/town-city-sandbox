## 1. What the game wants to be

A town stuffed with **lots of gameplay options**:

- Bar / brewery
- Combat
- Exploration and interactions
- Mini-games with progression systems
- Fun movement
- Lots of side story and quests

## 2. Core plan (build strategy)

- **Make the base game.** It can be simple, but it needs to be **scalable**.
- **Standardize core mechanisms** early: **money, time, reputation**.
- **Add side quests / stories / events** on top of that base.
- **Continue to flesh out the world** and develop the gameplay mechanics above.

*(This matches the ROADMAP's vertical-slice-first approach: a small, scalable base before going
wide.)*

## 3. Character types

Four kinds of beings populate the world:

| Type | Role |
|---|---|
| **Human** | NPCs, enemies, etc. |
| **Cartoon / strange characters** | Still NPCs, but non-human (e.g. Droghnaut the alien, Fredward the frog being). |
| **Animals** | Can be hunted and harvested. |
| **Monsters** | Combat enemies. |

## 4. NPC professions

Musician · Banker · Farmer · Shopkeeper / clerk · Clothes designer · Chef / restaurant staff ·
Tour guides / park rangers · Police · Government workers · Adventurers (maybe a guild for them) ·
Craftsman (tools, weapons, etc.) · Wizard (get spells and whatnot).

## 5. Industries within the town

Mining · Farming · Fishing · Tourism · Office work · Brewing · Rare item collection (artifacts, etc.).

## v1 subset (what makes the slice)

Per GDD §9, **v1 is a short game** that ships only a small slice of the above. **Locked 2026-06-28**
(full detail in GDD §9.6):

- **Locations:** town hub · **one** outlying wild area · **one** procedural dungeon at a **single
  distinct theme** · functional interiors: George's General Store, The Flaming Pebble, Kippie
  Arcade, Astros Fishing Friends, the Adventurers Guild.
- **Cast (~6):** Orbo Orland (mayor / story spine), George Coral (general store), Barry Barnson
  (bar), Kippie Kip (arcade), Droghnaut (fishing), Sally Steelfield (adventurers guild). *(Al
  Firestorm optional.)*
- **Mini-game (Pillar 3):** **Bartending @ The Flaming Pebble** — the one job built to full
  progression. Other jobs stay light.
- **Story:** a **"help the town" arc** led by Mayor Orbo. The Han de Seciro thread + larger plot
  are parked.

Everything else in this bible stays parked as vision.
