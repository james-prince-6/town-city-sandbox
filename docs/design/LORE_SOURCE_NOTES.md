## 2026-06-29 — The mine, the gems, the monsters, the gate

> Verbatim brain dump from James:

So this town was originally a mining village, it is set deep in the mountains of the country. The town boomed from iron copper, and gem mining. Especially the gems. They were special in that the gems contained magical power (generic non specific magic for now). As such a group of ancient wizards has actually lived in the area for millennia, using the gems for their magic (and immortality). To the layman, these gems are just regular rubys emeralds and such, still valuable but they are oblivious to their true use. So the town thrived on mining for many decades, with an eventual steady state group of miners (~5 or 6) staying permently in town. Although the town started off fixated on mining, it has since branched out into many other sectors, we can cover this later. So with the gems and all this magic, eventually monsters found them and started to eat them. since they are magical, they rely on the gems to survive and procreate. The monsters arrived in full force about 1 year ago, there had been sightings before but they were just urban legend. There was some event that triggered this most recent wave, which will be revealed throughout the story. I'm thinking that a gate was opened, by someone who seems good and is close to the town (maybe mayor). This gate connects to the monster world, and has since spread beyond the control of the instigator. mark this as a point to flesh out in the future, i think maybe the mayor was trying to revive the town economy, and found some ancient book that told him a spell to open the gate. from what he thought though it would just create more gems to mine. He keeps this secret until revealed.

## 2026-06-29 — Town economy branching out; old-guard tension

> Verbatim brain dump from James:

the town while once totally dependent on mining, has branched out into new sectors over the last decade. some of the old guard are resentful of these changes, and want to keep mining at the core of the town. Some of the changes include an increased tourism sector, lots of pleasure and leisure companies, one big tech firm, and all the other various businesses around.

## 2026-06-29 — Where the monsters come from; gem mechanic; the main arc

> Verbatim brain dump from James:

So the monsters come from this other realm, lets just call it hell for now. they are twisted creatures whos only desire is to kill and eat gems. In the dungeons there should be occasional gems, and if an enemy is able to reach it before you and eat it, they should go into a super state. Gems for the player should also do something similar, where the mana bar stays full for a given duration. the goal of fighting these monsters will be to find out where they came from, who is behind them, and how to stop them. there will be boss fights and several levels that need to be clear in order to reach the end boss, which will be in hell and be fighting the ultimate boss: balrog. All of this stuff ties in heavily with the wizard council.

## 2026-06-29 — Liu Fang (bowling alley owner)

> Verbatim brain dump from James:

Liu Fang is an older asian gentleman. He has a slight hunch, but pleasantly nice face. he is small and unassuming, but he is an absolute legend at bowling. He was the world record holder back in his day, however there was big drama at one of the tournaments and he had to drop from the scene. He now lives out his "exile" in town city, running the bowling alley. If you ask him nicely he'll play you, however his skills have not deminished one bit.

## 2026-06-29 — Fredward (ancient frog being)

> Verbatim brain dump from James:

Fredward is a mystical being. He has ties to the magical realms, but is deeply rooted in the land. He speaks in a funny froggish, yoda like way, and give vague advice on how to handle the monsters. He is deeply saddened by them and as a result has lost lots of his powers. He has deep resentment for the humans causing this, but will not act against them. In fact he does not act against anything, as he is more of a force of nature than an individual.

## 2026-06-29 — Han de Seciro's mission & the Balrog's heart (two-stage finale)

> Verbatim brain dump from James:

For han there will be much more to add, but i kind of want his mission to revolve around finding the hell gate, and retreiveing some kind of power from it for his kingdom. (big reveal can be that hes crown prince). What he is actually after, although he does not know it, is the balrogs heart, which is the essance of its being. Its an ultimate corruptor, so anyone who wields it actually becomes the balrog. I'm thinking this could be a fun stage two for the boss, after the balrog is killed for the first time, han picks up the heart and becomes the balrog, making you fight him again.
